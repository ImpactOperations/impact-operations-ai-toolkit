# Impact Operations AI Toolkit

Practical AI prompts and toolkits for not-for-profits, charities, and for purpose led organisations. Get more from your AI tools without needing to be technical. Built and maintained by Impact Operations.

Because those doing the most good deserve the most flow.

Maintained by [Impact Operations](https://impactoperations.ai).

---

## Why this exists

Purpose driven teams are stretched thin. Budgets are tight. The work is urgent. AI tools should be making this easier — but most of what's out there is built for tech companies, sales teams, or agencies. Not for the people running grant cycles, coordinating volunteers, drafting board papers, or holding the operations of a purpose-driven organisation together with goodwill and gaffer tape.

This repo is our attempt to fix that.

We're sharing the prompts, frameworks, and tools we use with our clients — and the ones we're still figuring out. Some are polished. Some are works in progress. All of them are designed for the realities of mission-led work.

If you're operating a purpose driven organisation and AI feels like one more thing to figure out alone, this is for you.

---

## How this works

This isn't a finished product. It's a working space.

- **We share what we build.** Prompts, frameworks, and toolkits go here as we develop them. Free to use, adapt, share.
- **We seek feedback.** What worked? What didn't? What's missing? Tell us — through issues, discussions, or directly.
- **We iterate.** Every prompt is versioned. When something improves, we update it. When something doesn't work, we say so.

The goal isn't perfection. It's operations as ambitious as your mission — built together, in the open, by the people who actually need them.

## How to use this repository

1. Browse the folders above. Each one is a self-contained toolkit.
2. Open the folder you're interested in.
3. Read the instructions (always called `instructions.md`).
4. Follow the steps. Every prompt is copy-paste — no setup required.

You don't need to install anything. You don't need a GitHub account. You just need access to ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini.

## What we believe

These principles shape every prompt in this repo:

- **Architecture before automation.** AI applied to broken processes accelerates failure. Get the structure right first.
- **The right problem first.** Tools should serve the work, not the other way around.
- **Adoption over delivery.** A prompt that doesn't get used is worthless, no matter how clever it is.
- **Equity by design.** Plain language, low cost, accessible structure. If it only works for technical users with paid plans, we've missed the point.
- **Built in the open.** The for-purpose sector grows when knowledge moves freely. We share what we know.
